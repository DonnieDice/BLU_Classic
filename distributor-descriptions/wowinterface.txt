BLU Classic - Better Level Up! - Classic Edition

The ultimate level-up notification addon for ALL World of Warcraft versions!

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(255, 204, 0)][B]Universal Compatibility[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
BLU Classic works seamlessly across EVERY World of Warcraft client:
• Retail (The War Within)
• Classic Era / Hardcore / Season of Discovery  
• Cataclysm Classic
• Wrath Classic
• Burning Crusade Classic
• Mists of Pandaria Classic

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(255, 204, 0)][B]Features[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
• [B]50+ Custom Sounds[/B] from legendary games like Zelda, Final Fantasy, Pokemon, Elden Ring, and more!
• [B]Individual Volume Controls[/B] for each notification type
• [B]Smart Version Detection[/B] - One addon, all WoW versions
• [B]Lightweight & Efficient[/B] - Minimal resource usage

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(255, 204, 0)][B]Notification Types[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
• Player Level Up
• Quest Completed
• Quest Accepted
• Achievement Earned
• Reputation Rank Up
• Honor Rank Up (Retail)
• Renown Level Up (Retail)
• Battle Pet Level Up (Retail/MoP)
• Post Activity Complete

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(255, 204, 0)][B]Commands[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[CODE]/blu[/CODE] - Open configuration panel
[CODE]/blu help[/CODE] - Show available commands
[CODE]/blu debug[/CODE] - Toggle debug mode
[CODE]/blu welcome[/CODE] - Show welcome message

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(255, 204, 0)][B]Installation[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
1. Download BLU Classic
2. Extract to your Interface/AddOns folder
3. Launch WoW and enjoy!
