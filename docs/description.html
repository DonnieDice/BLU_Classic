# 🎮 RGX | Better Level-Up! Classic ![BLU Classic Logo](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/donniedice/BLU_Classic/main/images/BLU_Logo.png) ## ![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/donniedice/SimpleQuestPlates/main/images/kiwi.gif) RGX Mods - RealmGX Community Project *** ## 🎯 Overview **RGX | Better Level-Up! Classic** is the universal version of BLU that brings customizable level-up sounds from 50+ games to World of Warcraft — across ALL versions, from Classic Era to Retail! ![RealmGX Kiwi](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/donniedice/SimpleQuestPlates/main/images/kiwi.gif) **The Kiwi Says:** "Works on ALL versions — because nostalgia knows no bounds! Bwwiiiee." *** ## ✨ Features 🏆 Achievements - Play sounds from other games when you earn Achievements! 🐾 Battle-Pets - Play sounds from other games when you level-up Battle-Pets! ⛏️ Delve Companion Level-Up - Play sounds from other games when your Delve Companion levels-up! (Retail only) ⚔️ Honor Ranks - Play sounds from other games when you gain Honor Ranks! 📈 Level-Up - Play sounds from other games when you Level-Up — on every WoW version! 🌟 Renown Reputation Ranks - Play sounds from other games when you gain Renown reputation ranks! 👥 Reputation Ranks - Play sounds from other games when you gain Reputation ranks! 📋 Quests - Play sounds from other games when you Accept or Turn-in Quests! 🏪 Trading Post Activities - Play sounds from other games when you complete Trading Post activities! (Retail only) 🎵 Soundpack Support - BLU Classic auto-discovers compatible sounds from other loaded addons at startup — no external libraries required. If LibSharedMedia-3.0 is installed, BLU Classic hooks into it automatically for broader coverage. 🗂️ User Custom Sounds - Drop your own .ogg, .mp3, or .wav files into BLU_Classic\user\sounds\ and register them in user\user_sounds.lua. They appear instantly as a selectable User Custom Sounds pack in every module's dropdown — no extra addons required! **Note:** All sounds feature independent volume adjustment. For Retail-only features, see [BLU](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/blu-better-level-up). *** ## 📋 Slash Commands The addon can be managed using the following slash commands:
/blu # Opens the options panel
/blu debug # Toggles debug mode
/blu welcome # Toggles the welcome message on login
/blu help # Displays help information
/blu refresh # Rebuilds external and user custom sound pack registry
/blu rescan # Rescans for newly registered media (no reload needed)

Classic from your preferred platform: * [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/blu-classic) * [GitHub](https://github.com/donniedice/BLU_Classic) * [Wago.io](https://addons.wago.io/addons/blu-classic) * [WoWInterface](https://www.wowinterface.com/downloads/info26465-BLU-Classic.html) 2. **Extract** to your WoW `Interface/AddOns` directory for your version: * **Retail WoW:** World of Warcraft/retail/Interface/AddOns * **Classic/Mists:** World of Warcraft/classic/Interface/AddOns * **Classic Era:** World of Warcraft/classic_era/Interface/AddOns 3. **Restart** WoW and enable the addon in the AddOns menu. ![RealmGX Kiwi](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/donniedice/SimpleQuestPlates/main/images/kiwi.gif) **The Kiwi Says:** "Works everywhere!
